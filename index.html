About Me

Hello there, I'm Nicki, though you can call me NickiBreeki if you'd like.
I'm an 18-year-old Asian gal with a knack for game programming, and currently, I'm immersed in the world of Roblox development.

I've been honing my programming skills for a good 5-7 years now, starting way back in 2012 when I was just 10.
It's been quite the journey, and along the way, I've developed a sharp eye for the finer details and a genuine passion for crafting games that truly captivate and entertain players.

Programming isn't my only talent, though! I've also dabbled in animation for about a year.
My primary focus is on animating weapons in R6 packages because I find the challenge of animating R15 to be quite invigorating.

When it comes to role models in the programming world, Nelson Sexton, aka SmartlyDressedGames, stands out as a big inspiration for me.
I aspire to follow in his footsteps and continue crafting innovative and captivating games that players will absolutely love.
